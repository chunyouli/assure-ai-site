I. Introduction

Assure AI LTD ("we," "us," or "the Platform") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of elderly individuals and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Elderly Care Robot SMS Response Service.

II. Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Account Information: When you register for a platform account, we may collect your name, mobile phone number, and email address.
  • Elderly Individual Information: To provide elderly care services, you may need to provide the elderly individual's name, age, health status (e.g., allergies, medication regimen), and emergency contact information.
  • Communication Content: The content of your SMS interactions with the elderly care robot, including inquiries, received reminders, and reply commands.

2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: This includes the mobile carrier associated with your phone number and device model (limited information obtained via SMS gateway).
  • Usage Data: SMS send/receive timestamps, interaction frequency, and feature usage records.

III. How We Use Your Information

1. Core Service Functions

  • Sending life assistance information to elderly individuals via SMS, such as medication reminders, activity alerts, and weather warnings.
  • Receiving SMS inquiries from elderly individuals (e.g., "What medication do I take today?" or "Emergency help") and responding via robot or human customer service.
  • Sending status notifications to family members or guardians (e.g., "Today's medication has been completed" or "Abnormal activity detected").

2. Service Optimization and Security

  • Analyzing interaction data to improve the accuracy of robot responses and service quality.
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or illegal activities to ensure platform security.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements and cooperating with regulatory investigations.

IV. Special Provisions for SMS Communication

1. User Consent

  • When an elderly individual or their guardian checks "Agree to receive SMS notifications" during registration, they are authorizing the platform to send service-related information via SMS.
  • Guardians may consent on behalf of the elderly individual to receive SMS, but must ensure they have obtained the elderly individual's oral or written authorization.

2. Opt-Out Method

  • Users may opt out of all marketing or notification SMS at any time by replying "STOP" (emergency assistance service notifications are excluded).
  • After opting out, the platform will cease sending non-essential service SMS but may retain necessary account security notifications.
We do not share, sell, or distribute your phone number or SMS consent information to any third parties under any circumstances.

V. Information Sharing and Disclosure

1. Sharing with Authorized Third Parties

  • We may share information with service providers that provide technical support (e.g., SMS gateway providers, cloud storage providers), but these third parties may only use your information for the purpose of providing services to us and are subject to strict confidentiality obligations.
  • When an elderly individual triggers an "Emergency Assistance" alert, we may share location information or health data with their pre-set emergency contacts (e.g., children, community service personnel).

2. Legal Requirements

We may disclose relevant information when required by law or to protect the life and safety of the elderly individual, user, or others.

VI. Data Security

  • We employ encryption during transmission, access controls, and regular backups to protect your personal information.
  • Sensitive information such as health data and location information of elderly individuals will be encrypted and stored separately, with access restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Please note that SMS communication carries an inherent risk of interception; we recommend that users avoid sending highly sensitive information (e.g., bank card passwords) via SMS.

VII. User Rights

  • Access and Correction: Users may view or modify their personal information through platform account settings.
  • Right to Deletion: Users may request deletion of their account and associated data, except for information required to be retained by laws and regulations.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Users may withdraw consent for SMS communication at any time by replying "STOP" or contacting platform customer service.

VIII. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be posted on the platform website or via SMS notification, and users will be notified through prominent means (e.g., pop-up window, email).
