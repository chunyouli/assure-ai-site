Our Service

SMS Consent & Terms

Users enter their phone number during registration and explicitly agree to receive SMS alerts related to service events such as medication reminders, wellness check-ins, and emergency notifications. Guardians may consent on behalf of an elderly individual provided they have obtained prior authorization.

Message frequency varies based on service activity. Standard message and data rates may apply. Users can opt out of non-essential notifications at any time by replying STOP, or request help by replying HELP.
